Movie Review: MaXXXine

July 14, 2024 2-minute read
Noah Pitts
Movie Review

MaXXXine Link to heading

MaXXXine is a 2024 thriller film written, directed, produced, and edited by Ti West. This is the thrid film in the trilogy, and I am a big fan of the first two. So much so, I decided to treat the night as a double feature, and watched Pearl beforehand. I was really excited walking into the theatre for this one!

Thoughts on MaXXXine Link to heading

I loved it.

Coming in at 104 minutes, it felt very well paced. Not a lot of wasted scenes and it was able to keep my attention throught its runtime. I’ve been telling all my people to go and check it out if you need soemthing to do on a Friday night.

How MaXXXine made me feel Link to heading

Inspired.

A recurring theme througout the trilogy is feeling like a STAR.

In this film, protagonist Maxine Minx is hungry, and is continuing her quest to becoming a STAR.

I also feel like im on a quest to feel like a STAR, so seeing her journey just really speaks to me in a way I cant fully explain (yet).

Final thoughts on MaXXXine Link to heading

Ti West has earned my respect.

All 3 films are very different from each other, but compliment each other well. While my favoirte film of the trilogy is still Pearl, all 3 films are very strong entries. I think a good sign of a good movie is passing the “will you watch it again?” test.

It passes - I’d watch all three films again.

Fun Facts Link to heading

While watching the end credits (I know i’m a sicko) - I noticed the lawyer & law office for the film was The Law Firm of Stacey A. Davis

Three cool things about Stacey and her Firm:

  1. She was listed in the credits twice since she is the owner and attorney at her office!

  2. Her office is headquarted in my hometown of Birmingham, Alabama! I would have never known I was so close to Hollywood growing up, but the world just gets smaller each day!

  3. She is an award winning film maker! I’m hoping to find a place to check out her film CHERRY - which is about a strait-laced mother of two getting high with her dying father on Easter Sunday.
